My Journey

I started my journey in technology with a curiosity about how things work. My first experience coding was printing "Hello World", however my first programing project that I decided to do was a noughts and crosses program. This was after learning the basics of python where I knew I had to implement my skill to refine and elevate my programming techniques. As I wasn't familiar with any coding language it was quite hard for me to keep up in lesson, as I was the only one who hadn't done computer science GCSE. This really brought my motivation down and almost made me drop the subject as a whole, however I buckled down and continued trying my best. Finally, I saw my grades slowing climbing up and so did my confidence in the course. After researching many careers in the industry by attending webinars, reaching out to people on social media and conducting my own research, I realised I needed to make myself more appealing and well-rounded in my knowledge. This lead me to start courses on SQL in Data Science, Excel, Software Development and Business Analysis. I really enjoy where I am at right now but also excited for what there is to come. Since then, I have been driven to learn more and progess alongside technology.
